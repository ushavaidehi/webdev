History of Saregamapa

Saregama India Ltd. It is India's oldest music label owned by RP- Sanjiv Goenka Group of companies.The company is listed on the NSE and BSE with its head office located in Kolkata and other offices in Mumbai, Delhi and Chennai. Apart from music, Saregama also produces films under the brand name Yoodlee Films and multi-language Television content.
